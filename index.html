THINGS TO DO IN
KANSAS CITY

FOOD

Joe's Kansas City BBQ
Jack Stack BBQ
Arthur Bryant's BBQ

Though Kansas City is known for having the country's best barbeque, one can also find a wide range of other cuisines, ranging from steakhouses to Asian.

MUSEUMS

Nelson Atkins Museum of Art
National World War 1 Museum
Steamboat Arabia Museum

There is no shortage of cultural enrichment, no matter if you're a history geek or an art connoisseur. Prepare to learn and be amazed.

LANDMARKS

Liberty Memorial
Union Station
The Plaza District

Everything from historic art-deco buildings to unique shopping districts can be found in Kansas City. Don't miss the opportunity to go to the top of Liberty Memorial for a bird's eye view of the city.

SPORTS

Kansas City Royals
Kansas City Chiefs
Sporting KC

Home to the 2015 World Series Champion Royals, Kansas Citians will cheer for the home team through both winning and losing seasons.

OUTDOORS

Kansas City Zoo
Worlds of Fun
Swope Park

There are tons of fun things to do outside in Kansas City. Want to explore the wilderness? We've got you covered. Want to experience the thrills of a roller-coaster? We've got that too.
